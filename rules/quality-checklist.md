# Article Quality Checklist

Run this checklist before marking an article `status: published`.

## Mandatory

- [ ] Frontmatter is complete and valid (all required fields present)
- [ ] Lead paragraph: 2–4 sentences, self-contained, no jargon without definition
- [ ] All acronyms spelled out on first use
- [ ] At least one `## References` entry with source document and section
- [ ] `## See Also` section with at least two internal links
- [ ] All `[[wikilinks]]` resolve to existing files
- [ ] No promotional language or first/second person
- [ ] Terms used that exist in the glossary are linked

## Recommended

- [ ] Relevant SDG goals cross-referenced
- [ ] Relationship to ESRS/CSRD/EU-Taxonomy noted where applicable
- [ ] A summary table or key facts box for standards articles
- [ ] Article reviewed against `writing-style.md` and `term-definition.md`

## For Glossary Terms Specifically

- [ ] Definition follows genus + differentia structure
- [ ] Source of definition is normative (regulatory text preferred)
- [ ] Synonyms and abbreviations recorded
- [ ] Domain tag assigned
